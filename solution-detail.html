How the System Works

1

Data Collection & Monitoring

The system continuously collects data from various sensors, cameras, and monitoring devices deployed across the facility. Real-time data streams are processed and analyzed to detect anomalies, security threats, or operational issues.

2

Intelligent Analysis & Processing

Advanced AI and machine learning algorithms analyze the collected data to identify patterns, predict potential issues, and generate actionable insights. The system can distinguish between normal operations and potential threats or anomalies.

3

Alert & Notification System

When critical events are detected, the system immediately generates alerts and notifications to relevant personnel through multiple channels including mobile apps, email, SMS, and centralized monitoring dashboards.
